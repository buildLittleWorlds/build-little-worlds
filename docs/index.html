Build Little Worlds

This site is a slow experiment in studying a scholar's work one small object at a time. For now the scholar is Philip Rieff, and the objects are deliberately modest: a citation, a paper idea, a paragraph, a question that can bear more weight later.

About the method

Small pieces before systems

The premise is simple: a scholar's work can be explored by building tiny, inspectable worlds around its ideas. A citation is one world. A paragraph is another. A paper idea is a third. Over time, these pieces should become a richer map of Rieff's vocabulary, tensions, and intellectual afterlives.
